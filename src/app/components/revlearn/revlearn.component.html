RevLearn

About

RevLearn is an educational platform that empowers students to explore, learn, and connect. Here's what you can do on our platform:

  • Register and log in as a student.
  • Enroll in courses tailored to your interests.
  • Take assigned quizzes to test your knowledge.
  • Keep track of your grades with the integrated gradebook.
  • Engage in meaningful discussions with your peers through our forum.
  • Edit your personal information to keep your profile up to date.
  • Conveniently pay for your classes from your account page.

Journey

Our journey with RevLearn was a collaborative effort involving a team of five. We began with discussions about the Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) to map out how different entities would connect. We then created wireframes to visualize the final product. Along the way, we made tough decisions to refine the project scope. Our project was completed in just two weeks. To stay organized, we used GitHub Projects to manage our progress.

My Role

During my involvement in the RevLearn project, I took on several key responsibilities:

  • Implemented the Quiz entity's layers, including the model, controller, service, and repository layers, using Spring Boot, Spring ORM, and Spring MVC.
  • Efficiently utilized GitHub Projects as a task management tool, ensuring effective task assignment and tracking for the development team. We conducted daily standup meetings to discuss progress, identify areas for improvement, and set goals.
  • Developed Angular components and methods to handle requests to the database, enabling seamless persistence of quizzes for each course.
